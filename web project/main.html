Umm Al-Qura University

Umm Al-Qura University (UQU) is a prominent Islamic university located in Mecca, Saudi Arabia. Established in 1949, it has since grown to become one of the leading higher education institutions in the region. The university is named after the Islamic calendar, "Umm Al-Qura," which means "Mother of All Cities. UQU started primarily as an Islamic university offering degrees in Islamic Law and Arabic language studies. It now offers more courses in such diverse subjects as Technology Management, Business Management, Islamic Economics, Marketing, Engineering, Technology, Medicine, Education, Architecture, as well various Applied, Social and Engineering Sciences."
